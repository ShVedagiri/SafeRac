Our Purpose + Outlook

Every year, millions of people are in danger or feel threatened in ride shares or walking in public, especially women. The risk of stalking or predators is extremely high and a dangerous situation to be in. SafeRec provides communication resources to reach out for help or to fake conversations that may discourage stalkers/predators.

Though we have not created a functioning app yet, our idea can be efficiently turned into a usable app. If our template comes to life, it can help people feel safe and potentially save lives.
